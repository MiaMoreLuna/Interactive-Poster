There's currently 15 to 51 trillion pieces of plastic in the worlds ocean.

Polystyrene, black plastics, #3 PVC and #7 PC plastics.

Seabirds, whales, fish and turtles mistake plastics for food and eventually die of starvation, stomachs full of plastics.
